What's-a Papa U-ey's?

Papa U-ey's is a family owned Orlando based Pizza Styled Food Truck. We offer classic pizza selections, made to order topped pizzas, Gluten-Free options, calizones, salads, and specialty deserts.

If you'd like to have your party or event catered by Papa U-ey's we only ask for a minimun 4 day notice and will be happy to be a part of making your next event pizza-tastic!
